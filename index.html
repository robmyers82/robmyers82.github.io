Hey, thanks for stopping by! I'm Rob, a full stack developer with experience developing products using both the LAMP and MEAN stack frameworks. I love solving problems and working out solutions for complex issues, so coding is naturally a passion of mine.

I currently reside in Sydney, Nova Scotia, Canada, where I run a Professional Development Training company, as well as an online ordering and delivery company. In the past, I served as Program Manager of the UIT Startup Immersion Program. Working with UIT allowed me to contribute to the local economy and workforce by providing prospective programmers with the mentors and resources that they need to learn the newest web and app technologies, and be a force in the programming and development community.

When not writing code, I can be found cycling, playing baseball, hanging out at the beach, or walking/running/hanging out with my dog and cat, Summer and Zelda.

MyBallLeague

A React and Node-powered app to easily manage and update a casual baseball or softball league.

MyBallLeague.com is a personal project that stemmed from a need to manage a local casual softball league. By using the MyBallLeague app, our softball committee can easily add and upload games, add and update scores, and manage rules, fields, schedule and playoffs.

For it's most recent rework, the site has been completely re-developed using a Sails.js back-end and React.js front-end solution. Future features include an online store and automated score submission solutions.
